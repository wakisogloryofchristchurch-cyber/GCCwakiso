Who We Are

About Glory of Christ Church

A Spirit-filled, Christ-centered community in the heart of Wakiso-Kayunga, Uganda.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To know Christ and to make Him known — building lives through worship, the Word, and Spirit-led outreach in our community and beyond.

👁️ Our Vision

To reveal the Glory of Christ and His finished work on the Cross, raising disciples who impact Wakiso-Kayunga and all of Uganda for God's kingdom.

📖 Our Belief

We believe in the authority of Scripture, the saving grace of Jesus Christ, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the call to love our neighbours as ourselves.

🤝 Our Community

Glory of Christ Church is a welcoming family where all are accepted, discipled, and empowered to live victoriously in Christ — regardless of background.

What Drives Us
