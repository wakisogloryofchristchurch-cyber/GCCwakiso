Welcome to Our Kids Ministry!

At Glory of Christ Church, we believe children are a precious gift from God. Our Kids Ministry is dedicated to creating a safe, fun, and engaging environment where children can learn about God's love, develop meaningful friendships, and grow in their faith journey.

Safe Environment

All our volunteers are background-checked and trained to provide the highest level of care and safety for your children.

Age-Appropriate Learning

We use creative Bible teaching methods suitable for different age groups, from toddlers to pre-teens.

Fun & Interactive

Learning about Jesus should be fun! We incorporate games, music, and crafts into every session.
