About Our Church

A Christ-Centered Community

Glory of Christ Church Wakiso-Kayunga is a Spirit-filled, Christ-centered community dedicated to worship, teaching, and outreach. We welcome everyone to join us in fellowship and service as we pursue God together.

Under the leadership of Pastor Harriet Ndagire Bagonza, we are committed to building lives through the Word of God and empowering believers to impact their communities.
