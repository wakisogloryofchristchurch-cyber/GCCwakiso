Our Regular Services

Join us for worship, prayer, and spiritual growth throughout the week

Sunday Main Service

Sundays at 9:00 AM

Our main weekly worship service with powerful praise, worship, and the teaching of God's Word. Experience God's presence and connect with our church family.

  • Anointed praise and worship
  • Bible-based preaching
  • Prayer and altar call
  • Children's ministry available
  • Fellowship after service
Main Sanctuary

Tuesday Prayer Service

Tuesdays at 5:00 PM

A dedicated time for corporate prayer where we intercede for the church, community, and nations. Experience the power of prayer and see God move.

  • Corporate intercession
  • Personal prayer needs
  • National concerns
  • Spiritual warfare
  • Miracles and breakthroughs
Main Sanctuary

Wednesday Bible Study

Wednesdays at 5:00 PM

Deep dive into God's Word with practical teaching and interactive discussion. Grow in your understanding of Scripture and Christian living.

  • Verse-by-verse teaching
  • Q&A and discussion
  • Practical application
  • Discipleship training
  • Small group interaction
Main Sanctuary

Friday Overnight Prayers

Fridays at 9:00 PM

A powerful all-night prayer vigil where we seek God's face for breakthroughs, miracles, and spiritual refreshing. Experience God's presence through the night.

  • All-night prayer vigil
  • Spiritual refreshing
  • Miracles and healings
  • Corporate worship
  • Breakthrough at dawn
Main Sanctuary

What to Expect When You Visit

We want you to feel welcome and comfortable when you join us

Warm Welcome

Our greeters will welcome you with a smile and help you find your way around our church facilities.

Spirit-filled Worship

Experience powerful praise and worship that ushers you into God's presence and prepares your heart for the Word.

Biblical Preaching

Hear relevant, practical messages from God's Word that apply to everyday life and spiritual growth.

Children's Ministry

Safe, age-appropriate programs for children during all services so the whole family can worship.

Fellowship

Connect with others over refreshments after service and build meaningful relationships.

Prayer Ministry

Receive prayer for your needs at the altar or from our prayer team members.
